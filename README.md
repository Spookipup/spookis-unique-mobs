# Spooki's Unique Mobs

42 mob variants for Minecraft 1.20.1 (Fabric). They show up naturally in the world mixed in with vanilla mobs.

Needs [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api). Drop the jar in your `mods` folder.

## Config

The config file is `config/unique-mobs.json`. Right now you can:

- Turn any mob on or off.
- Change the global spawn weight multiplier for all mobs.
- Change each mob's individual spawn weight multiplier.
- Change dungeon spawner replacement chance.
- Change themed dungeon spawner replacement chance.
- Change mineshaft replacement chance.
- Change nether fortress blaze spawner replacement chance.

## Mobs

### Zombies
- **Venomous Zombie** - poison on hit, death cloud. Jungles, swamps, caves.
- **Infernal Zombie** - fire guy, fights piglins. Hot biomes, nether.
- **Frozen Zombie** - freezes water, slows you on hit. Cold biomes.
- **Ender Zombie** - teleports when hurt, dies in water. Rare in overworld, common in end.
- **Wither Zombie** - lobs skulls, tracks two targets at once. Deep caves, nether.
- **Sprinter Zombie** - fast and fragile, lunges at you. Doesn't burn. Overworld.
- **Builder Zombie** - mines through walls, builds bridges to get to you. Overworld.
- **Plague Zombie** - withers animals nearby, buffs zombies when it dies. Jungles, swamps, caves.
- **Armored Zombie** - tanky. That's about it. Overworld.

### Skeletons
- **Sniper Skeleton** - hangs way back, hits hard, remembers you longer than normal. Overworld.
- **Ember Skeleton** - flaming arrows, fights piglins. Hot biomes, nether.
- **Ender Skeleton** - shoot, teleport, repeat. Rare in overworld, common in end.
- **Poison Skeleton** - climbs trees, shoots poison arrows from up high. Jungles, forests, swamps.
- **Multishot Skeleton** - 3 arrows per shot. Overworld.

### Spiders
- **Spitting Spider** - spits poison, circles at range. Jungles, swamps, caves.
- **Wither Spider** - wither on hit, spawns cave spiders when killed. Deep caves, nether.
- **Magma Spider** - turns stone to temp magma blocks, sets things on fire. Hot biomes.
- **Ice Spider** - pelts freezing snowballs then rushes when you're frozen. Cold biomes.
- **Jumping Spider** - tiny pouncing spider, spawns in trees. Jungles, forests.
- **Web Spinner Spider** - shoots webs, rushes when you're stuck. Swamps, caves.

### Creepers
- **Toxic Creeper** - explodes into a poison cloud. Jungles, swamps, caves.
- **Wither Creeper** - big boom + wither. Slow fuse so you hear it coming. Deep caves, nether.
- **Sculk Creeper** - tiny explosion, summons a warden. The creeper is the siren. Deep dark.
- **Magma Creeper** - turns blocks to magma on detonation. Hot biomes.
- **Frost Creeper** - ice explosion, bigger radius in cold biomes. Cold biomes.
- **Ender Creeper** - teleports to you mid-fuse. Rare in overworld, common in end.
- **Lightning Creeper** - explosion calls down lightning. Overworld.
- **Burrowing Creeper** - digs underground, tunnels behind you, surfaces and blows up. Overworld.

### Endermen
- **Assassin Enderman** - warps behind you, leaves damage clouds everywhere it teleports from. Rare in overworld, end.
- **Enraged Enderman** - always hostile, uppercuts you and teleports to your landing spot. Rare in overworld, end.

### Blazes
- **Blast Blaze** - gets in close, charges up, then pops a fiery blast that launches you. Nether fortresses.
- **Storm Blaze** - marks where you're moving and fires lightning straight at that spot. Nether fortresses.
- **Wither Blaze** - fires wither ash bolts, drops ash clouds, gets nastier inside them. Nether fortresses.
- **Soul Blaze** - sweeps soul fire breath and leaves burning trails behind. Nether fortresses.
- **Brand Blaze** - brands you from range, can save and buff nearby hostile mobs. Nether fortresses, bastions.

### Ghasts
- **Great Mother Ghast** - spawns ragelings instead of firing fireballs. Its spawned ragelings have a limited lifespan. Nether wastes, crimson forests.
- **Delta Ghast** - long charge, then a tracking beam that burns through blocks. Basalt deltas, nether.
- **Wither Ghast** - tractor beam pulls you in, then bites or drags you toward lava. Soul sand valleys, nether.
- **Rageling** - tiny attack ghast. Burns on contact, explodes when it's low. Nether wastes, crimson forests.
- **Skitterling** - dashes around dropping soul fire, then hits you with soul flame breath. Soul sand valleys, nether.
- **Obsidling** - armored dash ghast that tries to knock you into lava or off ledges. Basalt deltas, nether.
- **Blightling** - side-steps into firing lanes and volleys blight spores at you. Warped and crimson forests, nether.
